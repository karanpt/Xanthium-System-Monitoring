Welcome to Xanthium!

This website aims to provide a way of monitoring the System Cluster.
Xanthium is scalable distributed monitoring system for medium performance computing system such as clusters and grids.It uses technology like html,php,java and mysql.

Oil and natural gas corporation(ONGC) is the national oil and gas companny in India which is engaged in exploration and production activities both on shore and off shore.
We currently have 4 machines and are providing the analysis in 4 ways

  1. Current Hour
  2. Past Hour
  3. Past Day
  4. All Time Average

Hope you have an easy time monitoring the Cluster!
